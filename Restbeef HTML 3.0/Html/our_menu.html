About Us

817 Sports Bar & Grill is an all-new elevated sports entertainment venue located at 817 Queen St West in the ultra vibrant West-Queen-West area of downtown Toronto. This new space features a multitude of high-definition screens throughout the venue including 3 main large viewing screens, a sound system that gives one the feeling of experiencing a game live in the stadium. With 17 beers on tap served at -2C plus a variety of rotating seasonal beers, there is always something to quench your game day thirst. A great selection of spirits, whiskeys, and cocktails are sure to wet any appetite. A great new food menu has been developed. Good Food - Good Friends - Good Times.
